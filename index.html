Questions

Common Questions

What makes QuivaPro different from other agencies?
We blend deep Nigerian cultural intelligence with global luxury standards. Our work is not templated — every brand we build is rooted in original research and a strategy designed specifically for your growth trajectory.
How long does a typical branding project take?
Brand identity projects typically run Four–Six weeks. Full brand strategy engagements span Eight–Twelve weeks. Digital products vary from Six to Sixteen weeks depending on scope.
Do you work with early-stage startups?
Yes — some of our most exciting work has been with founders building from scratch. What matters to us isn't the stage, it's the ambition.
What industries do you specialise in?
We work across fintech, luxury goods, hospitality, real estate, fashion, health & wellness, and FMCG. Our cross-sector experience brings unexpected insight to every category.
How much does a branding project cost?
Brand identity starts from ₦2.5M; full strategy and identity from ₦6M. Book a call and we'll provide a detailed proposal at no cost.
Do you offer ongoing brand management after delivery?
Yes. We offer Brand Retainer packages for ongoing creative support — from campaign art direction to quarterly brand audits.
Can you work with international clients outside Nigeria?
Absolutely. While we are proudly Nigeria-based, roughly a third of our portfolio is international — UK, UAE, Ghana, Kenya, and the US.
What does the onboarding process look like?
After your discovery call, we produce a Brand Brief and proposal within 5 business days, then kick off with a deep-dive Brand Discovery Workshop.
How many revision rounds are included?
All projects include two structured revision rounds per deliverable, built into the timeline. Additional rounds can be scoped if needed.
Do you provide brand guidelines as part of delivery?
Yes — every brand identity project includes a comprehensive Brand Guidelines document covering logo usage, typography, colour systems, photography direction, and tone of voice.
